Why I am Osiris

I started using the name 'Osiris' online in the summer of 2017. I was using a children's coding website called 'Scratch', and I felt like I could create awesome things and gain more followers if I cleaned up my profile. I couldn't bring myself to delete my old projects, so I created a new account. I named it after the ancient Egyptian god of rebirth to symbolize my new start. If all Osiris and -Osiris- usernames are taken on a website, then I resort to using Osiris Rex as my name, like that of the space probe.
